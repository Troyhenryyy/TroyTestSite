Tracy Tousey, Executive Director


Tracy’s fascination with the social sector began during a college internship at the Z. Smith Reynolds Foundation. After a few years of practicing law, she returned to ZSR to work as a Program Officer. Later, she moved to Jacksonville to serve as Vice President of Community Initiatives for The Community Foundation for Northeast Florida. Tracy loves working with others to solve shared challenges. She currently volunteers with the Jacksonville Public Education Fund, the local library system, and a handful of other local nonprofit organizations.


          

Reed Colley, Co-Founder/Trustee


For more than a decade, Reed has developed business strategies and web-based applications for the advisory market. At age 26, Reed taught himself to code and went to work building an investment management platform. Reed founded Black Diamond Performance Reporting, and served as CEO until the company was acquired by Advent Software in 2011. He currently works as Senior Vice President, Acquisitions and Strategy at Advent, and is involved in the formation of a new technology startup called Flight Path.


          

Katie Colley, Co-Founder/Trustee


After working as a litigation associate, Katie hung up her law degree to join Black Diamond as Philanthropy Director. By creating a philanthropic culture that encouraged employees to highlight their own charitable passion, Katie witnessed first-hand the limitless power of personal connection and giving. Today, Katie supports a number of causes including gender equality, maternal health in third-world countries, and kids in need. Katie is also a board member of Wide Horizons for Children. She and Reed live in San Francisco with their four children.
